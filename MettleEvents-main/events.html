Rules and Regulations

Format

  • The game consists of a total of 3 levels.
  • Participants will be divided into groups of 5 members in the beginning of each level.
  • There will be one participant in the group designated as a sketch person during each round under a level, who will be making the sketch.
  • Each participant will be the sketch person once in a level.
  • 1st level: the participants will get very easy and basic terms related to metallurgy, which are easy to understand and guess. The sketch person will be given a choice to choose the word.
  • 2nd level: It will be of intermediate difficulty. The sketch person cannot choose the word in this round.
  • 3rd level: This round comes with an increased difficulty level. Here also the sketch person will not get a choice to choose the word.

Rules

  • The sketch person will get 20 seconds to think on the drawing / sketch of the word.
  • In the 1st round the participants will be given 60 seconds to guess the word. They will have to type the word in the chat box. Also, the sketch person will get a choice to select / choose the word.
  • In the 2nd round the participants will be given 50 seconds to guess the word. But the sketch person will not get a choice to choose the word.
  • In the 3rd round the participants will be given 40 seconds to guess the word.
  • Any participant found engaged in malpractices during the game will be eliminated.
  • Any decision by the event manager will be the final decision.

Judging Criteria

  • The first participant to guess the correct word will get 5 points and 1 bonus point if he/she guesses it within the first 20 seconds.
  • The sketch person will get 2 points if and only if the word is guessed within the first 20 seconds of the guessing time.
  • The top 2 scorers of the 1st level from each group will be shortlisted for the 2nd level.
  • Around 9 - 10 participants will be shortlisted for the 3rd level.
  • Finally, the top 3 scorers from 3rd level will be awarded.
