Events

Practical experience fusioned with theoritical understanding forms the complete education. Mettle presentes you a plethora of workshops on different emerging and eastablished technologies which help to gain a complete know how of engineering principles.

Coding Related Problem in Meta

A novel competition to test metallurgical reasoning and data analytics skills among the participants. We pose challenges to participants and provide them with data to come up with models based on machine learning algorithms. The event is hosted on Kaggle and candidate with model with least error emerge as winners. Prize money: Rs.3000 (1500+1000+500)

IIM Quiz

It is an annual quiz conducted in association with Indian Institute of Metals. The event is a single round quiz competition where participants are tested on their metallurgical knowledge. The expected number of participations is usually around 50 people.

Register

Quiz in Association with Balls of Picasso

A generic quiz competition consisting of multiple rounds to attract students from different departments. It is conducted in association with Balls by Picasso, the official quiz club of NIT-Trichy. There is one preliminary round to filter best teams and 4 rounds of finals to select the winners. Prize money: Rs.5000 (2500+1500+1000)

Register

Pictionary On Metallurgy Words

A fun event to engage students with the metallurgical version of the infamous event Pictionary. The event is to participated in teams of 3 and the winning team has to guess maximum number of words from the pictures they are presented. Prize money: Rs.2000 (1000+750+250)

Register

What If

The Essay Writing Contest is meant to attract Passionate Science Explorers and Material Science Enthusiasts. They express their creative train of thoughts through words backed by scientific principles based on given topics. Last year we received about 20 entries. Prize money: Rs.3000 (1500+1000+500)
