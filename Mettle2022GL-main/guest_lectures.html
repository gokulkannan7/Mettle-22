PROF RAMAMOORTHY RAMESH

SKYRMIONS: A NEW ERA IN FERROELECTRICS

With over 65,000 citations, our distinguished guest is a pioneer in the field of manganites and electric field control of ferromagnetism. Dr Wei Xiong

Calphad based Metallurgical Design for Alloy Manufacturing

Integrated Computational Materials Engineering (ICME) has become a workhorse for optimising the design of materials for future applications. One tool in this vastly powerful approach is the CALPHAD method, which helps in identifying the thermodynamics and kinetics of different metallurgical systems. Move a step high, as Dr. Wei Xiong, from the University of Pittsburgh, explores the ins and outs of the ICME approach applied to the burgeoning field of additive manufacturing.
